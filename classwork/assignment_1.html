Curve of Forgetting:

This article pertains to memory retainment and the importance of regular review when learning new concepts.

If you don't do anything with the information you've learned, the next day you lose more than half of it. By a week, you retain nearly none of it. By reviewing regularly, as little as 5 minutes a day, you'll be able to more easily remember the information you've learned weeks or months down the line.
